This example shows a simple data visualization based on open data from the [Open Data Portal of Ukraine](https://data.gov.ua/). The chart displays the distribution of higher education institutions (HEIs) in Ukraine by ownership type: state, communal, and private.  

This bar chart illustrates how higher education institutions in Ukraine are distributed by ownership type.  

- **State-owned HEIs** form the majority.  
- **Communal HEIs** represent institutions owned by local communities.  
- **Private HEIs** make up a significant portion of the higher education landscape.  

The data was retrieved from the official register of HEIs.
